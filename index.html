Subteams

LASSAT is proud to have members from a wide range of programs and disciplines, ranging from Space Engineering and Computer Science, to Digital Media and Statistics. Regardless of skill level, we are always excited to welcome new members. Our hope is for every member of LASSAT to leave with new knowledge and skills that they can apply to their future endeavours. Some of our subteams and their work packages are as follows.

Payload Implementation

Configuring and Implementing the payload for use with our OBC and transmittor

Attitude Control Systems

Designing and implementing magnetorquers to be used to fix satellite orientation in orbit

Mechanical Design

Designing the satellite structure within 3U spec

Onboard Power Design

Designing power PCB and solar panels to supply instruments with power

Communications

Designing and Building antenna system with methods to send and recieve packets

Software Development

Programming OBC using a Real Time Operating System to bring together onboard components
