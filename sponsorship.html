Sponsorship Information

Founded to compete in the annual Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC), Lassat was founded 4 years ago. With help from the Lassonde School of Engineering and York University, and with guidance from old team members and Lassonde professors, we are planning to compete again in CSDC-5.

We can not do this without help from our generous sponsors. If you are interested in sponsoring Lassat, please contact us here.
