About Us

Founded as CSDC@YorkU in 2014 after the competition this club was formed for, we have since grown into a hub for all things related to space technology. Our main focus has been, and always will be, to compete in the Canada-wide satellite design/building competition; CSDC. This makes us one of the only few clubs where various disciplines of engineering can come together to design and assemble a functional scientific intrument as an autonomous student group.

To that end, we provide the opportunity to learn, innovate, and connect with individuals with the same passion for the space environment and space technology from across Canada.
