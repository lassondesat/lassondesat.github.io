Sponsorship Information


We are starting a new design cycle to compete in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC). This competition allows university students from across Canada to design and create their own CubeSat from the ground up, with the winning team being awarded the opportunity to send their satellite into space and perform their objectives.

LASSAT depends on the Lassonde School of Engineering and York University, and guidance from senior team members and Lassonde professors, however, the team is planning to expand drastically during this design cycle, and require the generosity of sponsors to help kickstart our outreach program and grow our design team.

We are in contact with several schools and are hoping to create a catalog of workshops and talks that we can share with students from K-12. Our aim is to inspire a love for space and other STEM fields. You can read more about our Mission and our Outreach Program on our about page.

Official Partner

This year we are offering a special form of sponsorship with the Lassonde Satellite Design Team, and are inviting companies to become an Official Partner. The title of Official Partner is offered to the first 2 sponsors to donate $25,000 or more to LASSAT. Being an Official Partner gives you the unique opportunity to name the Satellite after your organization*, being our primary presenting sponsor at all local LASSAT hosted events, and more.

*Due to the nature of this benefit, only the first sponsor to qualify as an Official Partner will be able to name the satellite
